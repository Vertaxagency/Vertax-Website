FAQ

QUESTIONS WE GET
ALL THE TIME.

Straight answers. No fluff.

How much does Meta Ads management cost in Pakistan?+

Vertax Agency offers Meta Ads management starting from PKR 25,000 per month agency fee. The ad budget — typically PKR 15,000 to 30,000 per month — is paid directly by you to Meta and is completely separate from our management fee. Your full ad spend goes to running your campaigns, not to us.

How long does it take to see results from Meta Ads?+

Most clients start seeing measurable results within 2 to 4 weeks of launch. Week 1 is onboarding and strategy. Week 2 campaigns go live. By Weeks 3 and 4 we optimize and scale based on real data. We set realistic expectations from day one — no false promises, ever.

Do you offer a free audit? What does it include?+

Yes — completely free, no obligation. We review your current ad account or marketing setup, identify exactly where your budget is being wasted, and give you specific actionable recommendations you can use immediately. Even if you decide not to work with us, you leave with real value.

What is a good ROAS for eCommerce in Pakistan?+

For Pakistani eCommerce brands running Meta Ads, a ROAS of 3x to 5x is solid. 6x and above is excellent. Below 2x means strategy, targeting, or creative needs to be fixed. The benchmark also varies by industry — clothing, electronics, and food delivery all have different realistic targets.

Do you work with businesses outside Lahore?+

Yes. We work with businesses across Pakistan — Karachi, Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Faisalabad, and beyond. Everything is handled via WhatsApp and email with full transparency. You get weekly updates and a detailed monthly report no matter where you are in Pakistan.

What makes Vertax different from other digital agencies?+

Three things. You talk directly to Ali — the person actually managing your account. We focus only on restaurants and eCommerce so we know your market deeply. And we never make fake guarantees or promise specific numbers. We promise real strategy, complete honesty, and maximum effort on every account we take on.
